Made

Emmaus Cross

Third commission for the same school. The cross sits on the facade above the entrance, aligned with the path students walk every morning.

Anodised aluminium and glass. At night, a concealed LED strip edge-lights the glass.

Found

Structural colour

Butterfly wings create colour without pigment. Nano-scale structures interfere with light, producing iridescence that never fades.

Researchers at UCF have replicated this effect on aluminium. Paint that never fades because there's no pigment to degrade.

I've been watching this for years. Waiting for it to become available at architectural scale.
